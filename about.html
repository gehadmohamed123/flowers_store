About

Many people explain how High School can stifle a young mind's ability to create and truly discover themselves, and what they love...however, that's not the case with me. My first job was working at a small floral shop in my hometown of Stockton Missouri. I worked under the owner, whom to this day, I admire & aspire to create arrangements as intricate as hers. She taught me most everything she knew, and before too long, I discovered I was completely and entirely smitten by a life filled with florals, creativity, and ever changing project concepts. Today, I'm proud to hold my very own floral boutique, that each and every day progresses more into my vision. ​

The small details

It's the little moments that cause the biggest smiles, and the tiniest details that create the greatest bouquets. Each arrangement you find will be hand crafted, carefully analyzed, and styled to showcase the individual beauty of each flower in it's design. ​
